Edward Joseph Snowden is an American computer scientist and activist. After working in security for the National Security Agency,
he was recruited by the CIA to deal with computer security. In 2013, he was hired by an information technology company. In the same
year he revealed thousands of classified NSA documents to Guardian journalists, which revealed the existence of a mass surveillance
intelligence program around the world, therefore reporting violations of privacy, freedom of information and espionage networks.
The United States accused the computer scientist of crimes such as theft of government property and espionage, revoking his passport.
Since 2013 he has taken refuge in Russia, which garanted him citizenship in September 2022.
